FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? That's the first sign of a new beginning. Let's find the answers together.

Who is eligible to join The ASKT?

We welcome girls and women from all backgrounds and at any stage of life. Whether you're a school student who needs help with your studies, a young woman looking for your first job, a mother wanting to re-enter the workforce, or an entrepreneur with a business idea, there is a place for you in our ecosystem. There are no specific age or experience requirements to begin your journey with us.

I'm not sure which program is right for me. Can you help?

Of course! We know that every journey is unique. Our counselors are here to talk with you, understand your goals, and help you choose the perfect starting point. Weâ€™ll help you decide whether to begin with foundational skills in our LEARN program, get job-ready with EARN, or launch your venture in our LEAD incubator.

Will this program actually help me get a job?

Yes, absolutely. Our EARN program is built specifically to bridge the gap between learning and earning. We teach high-demand skills like Artificial Intelligence and Digital Marketing, but we don't stop there. Our dedicated job placement cell provides resume building, interview preparation, and direct connections to our network of corporate partners to ensure you have the best possible chance of landing a great job.

What makes The ASKT different from other online courses or coaching centers?

We are much more than just a training institute; we are a complete empowerment ecosystem. While other places might just teach you a skill, we provide end-to-end support for your entire journey. From foundational academics to landing a job, and even getting seed funding for your business, we are a lifelong partner in your success. You don't just join a course; you join a powerful community.

I have other responsibilities at home. Are the programs flexible?

We understand and respect the many roles women play. Our programs are designed with flexibility in mind. We offer a variety of class timings and learning formats that allow you to balance your education with your family, work, and personal commitments.
