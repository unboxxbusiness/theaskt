FAQs

Quick Answers

To help you save time, we've compiled answers to our most common questions about programs, partnerships, and how to get involved.

How do I apply for a program for myself or my daughter?

he best way to start is by exploring our Learn, Earn, and Lead program pages. Each page has details on how to apply. If you're still unsure where to begin, send us a message, and we'll be happy to guide you.

How can I make a financial contribution or donation?

Thank you for your generosity. To make a secure donation or learn about how your contribution creates an impact, please visit our Donation Page or contact our philanthropy team directly using the form.

Are you currently hiring staff or looking for volunteers?

We love to connect with passionate individuals who believe in our mission. You can find all current job openings and volunteer opportunities on our Careers & Volunteering Page.

I'm a journalist or member of the media. Who is the right contact?

For all press and media-related inquiries, please select "Media Inquiry" in the contact form's subject line for the fastest response. This will direct your message straight to our communications team.
